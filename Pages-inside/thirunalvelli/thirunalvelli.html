History of Tiruchirappalli

"Tiruchirappalli District is a district in the india the Indian state of Tamil Nadu and the administrative headquarters of Tiruchirappalli district. out of Hyderabad District."

Etymology

Tiruchirappalli's recorded history begins in the 3rd century BC, when it was under the rule of the Cholas. The city has also been ruled by the Pallavas, Pandyas, Vijayanagar Empire, Nayak Dynasty, the Carnatic state and the British. The most prominent historical monuments in Tiruchirappalli include the Rockfort at Teppakulam, the Ranganathaswamy temple at Srirangam dedicated to the reclining form of Hindu God.
